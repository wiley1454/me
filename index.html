About me

Hello, World ! My name is Olawale.

I’m a technology enthusiast, What matters most to me is building technology that solves large problems and empowers individuals / society at large, so I think a lot about Product Design, Product Management, Software Engineering, and Economics.

These days, I work as a Digital Designer at Amplify, My work spans across Visual/ Motion design and also Video editing with Figma and the Adobe Creative Suite.

I also enjoy building software with Python (Django), JavaScript, and HTML/CSS in my spare time.


I enjoy reading and researching about topics ranging from Cybersecurity, to Product Management due to my huge interest in understanding how certain things work in the tech ecosystem.

Previously, I configured routers/ switches at datacenters and installed microwave radios on cell towers at VDT Comms. I also provided IT Technical support to some commercial banks in Lagos during my time there.

And even before that, I also worked on creative visual design, and Advertising campaigns for brands such as Unilever, NairaBox, FreshYo, e.t.c. at Wildfusions .


In my free time, I listen to NPR’s Planet Money, The a16z Podcast and Lex Fridman's Podcast , build pet projects (software/ embedded systems), and play basketball.
